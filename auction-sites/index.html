Frequently Asked Questions

Are auction fees the same for everyone?

No. Most auctions (like BCA) have tiered accounts. "Standard" (Public) buyers pay the highest fees, whilst high-volume dealers with Gold/Black cards pay significantly less.

Do I pay VAT on buyer fees?

Yes. In the UK, you pay VAT (20%) on the buyer's fee, the online bidding fee, and any assurance/admin fees. This applies even if there is no VAT on the car itself.

What extra costs should I budget for?

Beyond the hammer price and buyer fee, budget for: V5/Indemnity fees (£25+), Online Bidding fees (£40+), Assurance reports (£40-£60), and delivery/transport.
